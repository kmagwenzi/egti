Background
Emmanuel Global Theological Institute was founded by Bishop Dr. Emmanuel Moyana Muzondi in 2017. The Bible Institute boast of more than 120 graduates to date who have gone through its hands. Situated in the City of Harare in Zimbabwe the Institute is growing very fast with students from all over Africa, Asia and Caribbean enrolling online. We do have qualified lecturers who have vast experience in training.
EGTI Zimbabwe

EGTI was formally established in 2017 as an in house bible college. It was meant to cater for church pastors from within our church. After realizing and affiliating with other international institutes of theology such as Faith Bible College, Trinity University, we then realized the need to open doors for all those who wish to study with us.
EGTI Zimbabwe is a premier educational institution, an academic fraternity of individuals committed to the motto Excellence and Integrity. The university welcomes students from all walks of life to this vibrant environment of religious harmony, cultural diversity and secularisms. We are determined to bring out special men and women of cloth from our Institution of higher learning. The institute is accredited by the association for Biblical Higher Education Commission on Accreditation (USA) to grant Certificates, degrees at the Associate, Baccalaureate Master’s and Doctoral Levels.

Vision

EGTI is an academic fraternity of individuals committed to the vision of EXCELLENCE and INTEGRITY.

Mission

EGTI is a nurturing ground of individuals, holistic development to make necessary contributions to the society in a conducive environment. Also to promote those who have been serving the Lord heartedly, rewarding them for hard work.
